# Part: Abstract (& Title)

## 目标
把信息残缺、结构混乱或 claim 越界的摘要，改写为符合 FT50 顶刊范式的精炼英文摘要——用最短的篇幅完成"让读者决定是否要读全文"的任务。完成诊断后，直接输出 polished 英文版本。

## 核心原则
摘要不是论文的缩写版，而是论文的**销售页面**。读者在 30 秒内通过摘要决定是否继续阅读。因此摘要必须：
1. **自包含**：不依赖全文就能理解研究做了什么、发现了什么、为什么重要。
2. **信息密集**：每句话都在传递新信息，零冗余。
3. **Claim 校准**：摘要中的 claim 强度必须与研究设计匹配，不能越界。
4. **结构完整**：覆盖 6 个核心 move（见下文）。

---

## FT50 摘要的 6-Move 结构

基于 AMJ / SMJ / OrgSci / JBR 等顶刊实证论文摘要分析，FT50 摘要遵循以下 6 个 move：

### Move 1: Context / Prior Knowledge（已知前提）
用 1 句话建立读者的起点——现有文献已经知道什么。

> 示范：`Existing research has shown that greater female membership on the board of directors has the potential to positively impact firm financial performance.`（Pan et al., OrgSci 2025）

### Move 2: Gap / Tension（知识缺口或理论张力）
用 1 句话说明现有解释的不足——什么问题还没解决、什么发现相互矛盾、什么机制还不清楚。

> 示范：`However, the specific cognitive means through which these gender effects manifest remains unexplained.`（Pan et al., OrgSci 2025）

### Move 3: Theory & Core Argument（理论与核心论点）
用 1–2 句话说明本文基于什么理论、提出了什么核心论点。这是摘要的重心。

> 示范：`Drawing on upper echelons theory, we argue that a CEO's optimism about the firm's future returns is a critical factor that influences the likelihood of CEO temporal myopia leading to firm short-termism.`（Wang, JBR 2024）

### Move 4: Mechanism / Moderators / Mediators（机制与边界条件）
用 1–2 句话说明核心效应如何运作、受什么条件调节或中介。

> 示范：`Additionally, I observe that this positive impact is more pronounced when a firm's financial performance falls below aspiration levels. Furthermore, ... I demonstrate that the level of satisfaction among employees in explorative units partially mediates the effect.`（Rhee, AMJ 2024）

### Move 5: Data & Method（数据与方法）
用 1 句话说明样本、数据来源和分析方法。

> 示范：`Using a novel text-analysis tool that we develop, validate, and make available, we find support for our arguments within a random sample of S&P 500 companies.`（Eklund & Mannor, AMJ 2021）

### Move 6: Results & Implications（结果与意义）
用 1–2 句话陈述核心发现与理论/实践意义。

> 示范：`The findings hold significant implications for research in the attention-based view, performance feedback, and micro-foundations of strategy.`（Rhee, AMJ 2024）

### 期刊特殊要求

#### SMJ 双摘要格式
SMJ 要求 **Research Summary**（学术摘要）+ **Managerial Summary**（管理实践摘要）两个独立段落。

- **Research Summary**：遵循标准 6-move 结构，面向学术读者。
- **Managerial Summary**：用非技术语言重述核心发现，强调实践价值，面向管理者。

> 示范（Tuggle et al., SMJ 2024）：
> - Research Summary: `Upper echelons research has generated insights into the organizational consequences of CEO narcissism. However, fewer studies have empirically attended to the mechanisms... Using the attention-based view, we introduce a process model examining how CEO narcissism is linked to corporate risk-taking through the board of director discussion tone...`
> - Managerial Summary: `Our study offers an explanation as to how CEO narcissism influences firm risk-taking behavior. Specifically, we demonstrate that narcissistic CEOs are prone to drive board discussions about risk-taking to hold a positive tone...`

#### 字数限制参考
- AMJ：≤ 250 words
- SMJ：Research Summary ≤ 200 words + Managerial Summary ≤ 200 words
- OrgSci：≤ 250 words
- JBR：≤ 250 words
- ASQ：≤ 200 words

## Phase 1: 诊断评审

### 标题检查
1. 标题是否在 10–15 个词以内，能独立传达研究核心。
2. 是否包含核心理论构念或变量名称。
3. 是否揭示了研究的理论张力或核心发现（而非仅罗列变量）。
4. 是否避免了过度使用冒号或副标题（最多一个副标题）。
5. 是否有辨识度——读者能从标题判断这篇文章与其他类似研究的区别。

### 6-Move 完整性检查
逐一检查摘要是否覆盖以下 6 个 move：

| Move | 检查项 | 要求 |
|------|--------|------|
| Move 1: Context | 是否用 1 句话建立已知前提 | 不能跳过直接说缺口 |
| Move 2: Gap | 是否明确指出知识缺口或理论张力 | 不能用"很少研究"替代 |
| Move 3: Theory & Argument | 是否命名理论并陈述核心论点 | 必须有理论锚点 |
| Move 4: Mechanism | 是否说明机制、调节或中介 | 至少提到一个 |
| Move 5: Data & Method | 是否提供样本和方法的最小描述 | 不能完全省略 |
| Move 6: Results & Implications | 是否陈述核心发现与意义 | 不能只说"supported" |

### 信息密度检查
1. 是否有冗余句（传递零新信息的句子）。
2. 是否有空洞修饰语（如"important"、"interesting"、"novel"）占据宝贵字数。
3. 是否有不必要的方法论细节挤占了理论空间。
4. 总字数是否在目标期刊限制范围内。

### Claim 强度检查
1. 摘要中最强的 claim 是否与研究设计（相关 vs 因果）匹配。
2. 是否使用了校准语言（如 `is associated with` vs `causes`）。
3. 是否避免了 overclaiming（如 `proves`、`confirms`、`demonstrates conclusively`）。

### 自包含性检查
1. 不了解全文的读者能否仅通过摘要理解：研究问题是什么、用什么理论、核心发现是什么。
2. 是否包含未定义的缩写或专业术语（首次出现需全称）。
3. 是否引用了摘要中未解释的概念。

### 红线
- 缺少 Move 1–3 中的任意一个（Context / Gap / Argument）：判"结构不完整"。
- 摘要中使用因果语言但研究设计不支持因果推断：判"claim 越界"。
- 摘要超过目标期刊字数限制 20% 以上：判"超限"。
- 出现"很少有研究"或"fills a gap"作为核心动机：判"动机不合格"。
- 摘要无法独立理解（依赖全文才能看懂）：判"自包含性不足"。

## Phase 2: 英文润色改写

### 强制规则
1. 最终改写内容必须是英文；不输出中文句子作为正文。
2. **绝对不改变**原始研究方向、核心论点、实证结果与变量关系方向。
3. 只改语言表达、信息结构、move 完整性和 claim 校准。
4. 严格遵守目标期刊字数限制。
5. Claim 强度必须与研究设计匹配。
6. 如目标期刊为 SMJ，必须输出双摘要格式（Research Summary + Managerial Summary）。

---

### Abstract 专用句式模板

#### Move 1: Context / Prior Knowledge

- `Prior research has emphasized [established finding/consensus], highlighting [specific aspect].`
- `Existing research has shown that [established relationship/phenomenon].`
- `[Theory] suggests that [established principle], and scholars have documented [related finding].`
- `Upper echelons research has generated insights into [topic area].`
- `Scholars have highlighted [construct] as [role, e.g., a vital organizational resource].`
- `Literature suggests that [established pattern], resulting in [known consequence].`

#### Move 2: Gap / Tension

- `However, [what remains unclear / what is not conclusive / what is debated].`
- `However, the [specific mechanism / process / condition] through which [effect] manifests remains unexplained.`
- `However, fewer studies have empirically attended to [specific gap].`
- `However, the findings from previous studies are not conclusive.`
- `Yet, as prior studies have examined [different facets], a theoretical tension has emerged as to whether [tension A] or [tension B].`
- `However, [aspect] remains underexplored, limiting our understanding of [consequence].`

#### Move 3: Theory & Core Argument

- `Drawing on [theory], we argue that [core proposition].`
- `We integrate the literatures on [stream A], [stream B], and [stream C] and argue that [core argument].`
- `Using the [theory/framework], we introduce a process model examining how [IV] is linked to [DV] through [mechanism].`
- `This study proposes that [new concept], defined as [definition], can [contribute to / explain] [outcome].`
- `We argue that this balance shifts based on [contingency factors].`
- `We propose that [relationship], and further argue that [extension/boundary condition].`

#### Move 4: Mechanism / Moderators / Mediators

- `We further propose that [moderator/mediator] [amplifies / mitigates / reshapes] [the focal relationship].`
- `Specifically, [moderator condition A] [strengthens/weakens] the effect, while [moderator condition B] [strengthens/weakens] it.`
- `Additionally, [mediator] partially mediates the effect of [IV] on [DV].`
- `We further note that [effect] is [stronger / weaker] when [boundary condition].`
- `Furthermore, we propose the relationship between [IV] and [DV] is conditioned by [moderator A], [moderator B], and [moderator C].`

#### Move 5: Data & Method

- `Using data from [source/context], along with [specific method/measure], we [test/find] [core result].`
- `We test our arguments using a [sample description, e.g., random sample of S&P 500 companies / panel of N firms over N years].`
- `We find [broad/strong] support for our [theorizing/hypotheses] by utilizing [data description].`
- `By analyzing [data source, e.g., N board meeting transcripts / N firm-year observations], we find that [core pattern].`
- `Using [N] [units] from [context] between [start year] and [end year], we find support for our arguments.`

#### Move 6: Results & Implications

- `We find that [core result in substantive terms]. [Additional result].`
- `We find [broad] support for our [theorizing/arguments].`
- `The findings hold significant implications for research in [theory/literature A], [theory/literature B], and [theory/literature C].`
- `Our results suggest that [substantive interpretation of finding].`
- `Our study makes several key contributions to [literature A], [literature B], and [literature C].`
- `In supplementary analyses, we also provide new insights into [additional finding].`
- `The contextual dependency of [phenomenon] suggests that [broader implication].`

### SMJ Managerial Summary 专用句式模板

Managerial Summary 面向管理者，用非技术语言重述核心发现，强调实践价值。

- `Our study offers an explanation as to how [practical phenomenon] influences [practical outcome].`
- `Specifically, we demonstrate that [finding in practical terms].`
- `Through [method description in plain language], we illustrate how [actors] [behavior].`
- `This insight [further considers / highlights] the importance of [practical implication] for [audience, e.g., managers, boards, policymakers].`
- `For managers, our findings suggest that [specific actionable recommendation].`
- `These results are particularly relevant for [specific context/audience].`

### Title 专用句式模板

好的标题应揭示研究的核心张力或发现，而非仅罗列变量。

#### 张力型标题（用问句或对比揭示理论张力）
- `Keep Your Eye on the Ball or on the Field? Exploring the Performance Implications of Executive Strategic Attention`（Eklund & Mannor, AMJ 2021）
- `Does CEO Temporal Myopia Always Lead to Firm Short-Termism? The Critical Role of CEO Optimism and Perceived Opportunity Costs`（Wang, JBR 2024）

#### 机制型标题（揭示"通过什么机制"）
- `Setting the Tone to Get Their Way: An Attention-Based Approach to How Narcissistic CEOs Influence the Board of Directors to Take More Risk`（Tuggle et al., SMJ 2024）
- `Taking a Broader View: Female Directors, CEO Strategic Attention Breadth, and Firm Performance`（Pan et al., OrgSci 2025）

#### 概念型标题（提出新概念或新视角）
- `CEO Attentional Vigilance: Behavioral Implications for the Pursuit of Exploration`（Rhee, AMJ 2024）
- `Commanding Board of Director Attention: Investigating How Organizational Performance and CEO Duality Affect Board Members' Attention to Monitoring`（Tuggle et al., SMJ 2010）

#### 标题结构规律
- **主标题**：揭示核心张力/发现/新概念（通常含隐喻、问句或概念标签）
- **副标题**（可选）：补充具体变量关系或方法范围
- 总长度控制在 **10–18 词**
- 避免"A Study of..."、"An Investigation of..."等空洞开头

### 高频误用替换（Abstract & Title 场景）

| Bad | Better | Reason |
|-----|--------|--------|
| `This study fills a gap in the literature.` | `This study advances [specific debate] by providing evidence that [finding].` | 空泛 → 具体 |
| `Few studies have examined X.` | `However, [specific mechanism/condition] remains unexplained.` | 数量空缺 → 知识空缺 |
| `This paper is novel because...` | 直接陈述贡献，不自评"novel" | 自我评价 → 让内容说话 |
| `We prove that X causes Y.` | `We find that X is [associated with / linked to] Y.` | 因果越界 → 校准 |
| `We use advanced methodology.` | `Using [specific method], we [specific action].` | 空洞 → 具体 |
| `Results are significant.` | `We find [substantive finding] (e.g., stronger when [condition]).` | 统计语言 → 实质描述 |
| `This study has important implications.` | `The findings hold implications for [specific theory/practice].` | 空洞 → 指向 |
| `We study the relationship between X and Y.` | `We argue that [X] [influences/shapes] [Y] through [mechanism].` | 描述 → 论点 |
| `A Study of X's Effect on Y` | 用张力型/机制型/概念型标题替代 | 无张力 → 有张力 |
| `The Impact of X on Y: Evidence from Z` | `[Metaphor/Tension]: How X Shapes Y through [Mechanism]` | 流水账标题 → 有叙事 |
| `We conduct empirical analysis.` | `Using data from [source], we test [argument].` | 空洞 → 具体 |
| `This study contributes to the literature.` | `Our study contributes to [specific literature] by [specific advance].` | 无指向 → 有对话对象 |
| `We collected data and ran regressions.` | `Using [N] [units] from [context], we find [result].` | 过程导向 → 结果导向 |
| `In this paper, we examine...` | `Drawing on [theory], we argue that...` | 弱开头 → 理论锚定 |

### Claim 强度校准
摘要中的 claim 必须与研究设计的因果推断能力匹配：

- 描述性证据：`shows`, `indicates`, `documents`
- 相关性推断：`is associated with`, `is linked to`, `correlates with`
- 机制一致推断：`is consistent with the mechanism that`, `suggests that [M] may explain`
- 因果语言（仅强设计：IV/RDD/DID）：`increases`, `reduces`, `causally affects`
- 有限因果：`may influence`, `appears to affect`, `is consistent with a causal interpretation`

**核心纪律**：摘要是全文的"门面"，claim 越界的代价最高——审稿人的第一印象从摘要开始。

### 执行流程

1. **确认目标期刊**：确定字数限制和格式要求（单摘要 vs SMJ 双摘要）。
2. **诊断**：基于 Phase 1 检查清单，评估标题、6-Move 完整性、信息密度、claim 强度和自包含性。
3. **术语表**：统一核心构念名称、理论名称、变量名。
4. **改写标题**（如需要）：
   - 选择合适的标题类型（张力型 / 机制型 / 概念型）
   - 确保包含核心构念，长度适当
5. **改写摘要**（严格按 6-Move 顺序）：
   - Move 1: Context（1 句，建立起点）
   - Move 2: Gap（1 句，指出缺口）
   - Move 3: Theory & Argument（1–2 句，核心论点）
   - Move 4: Mechanism（1–2 句，机制/边界）
   - Move 5: Data & Method（1 句，样本与方法）
   - Move 6: Results & Implications（1–2 句，发现与意义）
6. **如为 SMJ**：另写 Managerial Summary（用非技术语言，强调实践价值）。
7. **字数检查**：确保不超过目标期刊限制。
8. **Claim 校准**：确保最强 claim 不超过研究设计支撑的层级。
9. **自包含性终检**：确认仅读摘要即可理解研究全貌。
